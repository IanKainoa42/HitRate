Count every stunt rep. Know your hit rate.

HitRate is the stunt-outcome counter for cheerleading. Tap once per rep — hit, bobble, building fall, or major fall — and your dashboard builds itself.

FOR ATHLETES
Track the skills YOU'RE working on. Create your own list — lib, stretch, full up, anything — and log every rep at practice. Watch your hit rate climb across the season.

FOR COACHES
Switch to coach mode and track every stunt group on the floor. See which groups are locked in and which need reps, ranked automatically.

THE DASHBOARD
• Hit rate at a glance — today, this week, or all-time
• Trend line across your recent sessions
• Skills and groups ranked by hit rate, with movement vs last session
• Auto-written takeaways: what led the way, what to tighten next
• Session tape: every rep of your latest practice on one strip

STUNT CARDS
Turn your numbers into holographic trading cards. Earn milestone cards from COMMON to LEGENDARY — rarity is set by how hard each one is to earn, like 1,000 reps logged or 25 hits in a row. There are even DUBIOUS HONOR cards for the falls. Save them, copy them, or post them to Instagram.
