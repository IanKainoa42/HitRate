## 1. General-purpose pivot (positioning)
HitRate is NOT solely for cheerleading. Position as a general all-purpose
skill/trick outcome tracker — anyone working skills or tricks (skateboarding,
climbing, gymnastics, parkour, BMX, freestyle soccer) or **spectating**
(a parent/friend in the stands logging someone else's attempts). Cheer remains
the founder's home-turf vertical and launch beachhead; skate is the growth
wedge (see marketing/research/market-general.md).

Product already supports this: outcome names are user-renameable (severity
order/colors fixed) — Landed/Sketchy/Bail/Slam works as well as
Hit/Bobble/Building fall/Major fall.
