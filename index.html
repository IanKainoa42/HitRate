What is HitRate?

HitRate is a stunt-outcome counter for cheerleading. Log every hit, bobble, and fall during practice, and the app builds your dashboard — hit rate, trends, ranked skills, and holographic share cards. Track your own skills as an athlete, or track multiple stunt groups as a coach.
