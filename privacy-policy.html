HitRate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Overview

HitRate is designed with your privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

HitRate does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs, analytics services, or ad networks
  • No account creation or login required

Data Storage

All content you create in HitRate — skills, groups, practice sessions, and logged stunt reps — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Photos

If you choose to save a share card image, HitRate asks for permission to add the image to your photo library. The app never reads your existing photos, and saving happens entirely on your device.

Sharing

Sharing a card to Instagram or via the system share sheet is initiated only by you. HitRate hands the image you created to the app you choose; it does not transmit anything on its own.

Network Usage

HitRate makes zero network requests. The app functions entirely offline with no internet connection required.

Children's Privacy

HitRate does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
