## Benefit blocks

### Log fast
Four big buttons, one per outcome, full-surface tap targets. One hand, eyes up, haptic on every rep — log a whole session without looking down. Tapped the wrong one? One-tap undo. Walk off mid-session and the app picks right back up where you left off.

### See the truth
Your memory keeps the makes and forgets the bails. HitRate doesn't. Get an animated success rate, a trend line across your last sessions, and a leaderboard of your skills — plus takeaways written in plain words: your cleanest skill, your worst spot, and the one miss that's most fixable. The chart reads itself to you.

### Earn the card
Hit a milestone and unlock a holographic card — COMMON to LEGENDARY. Rarity isn't your score; it's how hard the milestone was to earn. 1,000 reps. 25 in a row. A perfect session. There are even DUBIOUS HONOR cards for the falls you survive. Tap to save the image, then post it wherever you want.

## Your sport, your words

The four outcomes are renamable, so the app speaks your gym's language. Severity order and colors stay fixed — you just change the words.

| Severity | Skate | Cheer | Gymnastics |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clean | Landed | Hit | Stuck |
| Rough but standing | Sketchy | Bobble | Stepped out |
| Down, minor | Bail | Building fall | Touched down |
| Down, hard | Slam | Major fall | Major fall |

Same four-level ladder. Your vocabulary on every screen — the log pad, the legends, the heatmap, the card.

## FAQ

**Is it free?**
That's the plan — free, with no ads and no tracking. Final pricing is still being set, but the model is "get out of your way and let you count."

**Does it work offline?**
Completely. Everything runs on the phone, so it works in a gym with no signal, at a park, anywhere. Nothing is uploaded. Your full log is exportable to a CSV any time, so your data is always yours to keep.

**Do I need an account?**
No. No sign-up, no login, no email. Open the app and start tapping.

**Can my coach or parent track me?**
Whoever's holding the phone does the tapping. A parent in the stands or a coach on the floor logs reps live on their own device — there's no remote monitoring, no shared cloud, no logging in to watch someone else. It's beautifully simple: tap what you see, where you're standing.

**What sports does it work for?**
Anything with attempts and outcomes. The outcome names are fully renamable, so it fits skateboarding, cheer, gymnastics, climbing, parkour, BMX, freestyle soccer — or whatever you're grinding. If you can land it, sketch it, or bail it, you can track it.
