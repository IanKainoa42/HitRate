# HitRate — Press Kit

## Boilerplate

HitRate is a one-tap outcome tracker for anyone grinding a trick or a skill. During a session you tap one of four big buttons per attempt — along a fixed best-to-worst severity ladder whose names you rename to fit your sport (Landed/Sketchy/Bail/Slam for skate, Hit/Bobble/Building fall/Major fall for cheer) — and the app turns those taps into a real success-rate dashboard with trend lines and plain-English takeaways. Hit a milestone and you unlock a holographic, trading-card-style collectible you can save and post, with rarity set by how hard the milestone was to earn. Everything lives on the phone: no accounts, no cloud, nothing leaves the device.

## Founder one-liner

HitRate is built by Ian Richardson, a solo iOS developer and cheer coach in San Diego who got tired of counting stunt hits in his head all practice — so he built a one-tap counter for it, then generalized it for anyone grinding any skill.

## Features

- One-tap logging: four full-surface buttons, one-handed, haptics per rep, instant undo, mid-session resume
- Renamable outcome ladder: your sport's words on every screen; severity order and colors stay fixed
- Success-rate dashboard: animated hit rate, distribution bar, trend line across sessions, color-coded rate bands
- Skills leaderboard that flips to an outcome heatmap
- Auto-written takeaways: best skill, worst-falls skill, most-fixable miss — in plain words
- Session tape: every rep in the latest session, with automatic rough-patch detection
- Holographic milestone cards: COMMON to LEGENDARY, rarity = milestone difficulty (incl. DUBIOUS HONOR fall cards)
- Save card image to Photos, copy to clipboard, or save-and-open Instagram to post
- CSV export of every logged attempt
- Athlete mode and coach mode (multi-group tracking)
- Fully offline, on-device only — no accounts, no tracking
